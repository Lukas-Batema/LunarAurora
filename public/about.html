LunarAurora

LunarAurora is a webproxy which started out as a fork of TitaniumNetwork's Alloy Proxy. Since then, it has had many updates, not just to the frontend, but the backend too. Additions such as Google Search and two new UI's, make it stronger that it has ever been.

Credits

  • Alloy for the initial framework.
  • Lukas Batema <lukasbatema@gmail.com> for all improvements and updates since our start.
