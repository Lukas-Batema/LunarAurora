LunarAurora

This is a webproxy which started out as a fork of TitaniumNetwork's Alloy Proxy, named LunarAurora. Since then, it has had many updates, not just to the frontend, but the backend too. Additions such as Google Search and two new UI's, made it stronger that it has ever been.

Credits
